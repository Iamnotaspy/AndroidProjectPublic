Controls
Touch and drag to use joystick and move the player ship.

After every few seconds a new projectile is shot from your ship. Once this hits the screen edge it will become dangerous to you and other ships and continuosly rebound.
Other space ships will move to dodge these projectiles.

You gain 10 points for outlasting another space ship. You gain 1 point for each projectile fired.

Once you are hit it's game over.

Level 1 has four normal enemies that just dodge.

Level 2 has two additional gravity enemies that periodically pull or push all projectiles away from themselves (indicated by how blue/red they are).

Level 3 has both the gravity enemies and a special enemy that is also able to fire projectiles.
